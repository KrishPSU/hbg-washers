🧼 How to Use the Washers

  1. Check machine availability on the main page and book a washer.
  2. Bring your laundry to the washer you booked.
  3. Load your clothes and add detergent (don’t overload it!).
  4. Press START on the washer within 5 minutes of booking.
  5. Come back when it's done — you'll get an email when the cycle finishes.
  6. Unload promptly so others can use the machine — be kind, be fast! 😊
